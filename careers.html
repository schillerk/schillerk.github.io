TAKE ON THE HARD PROBLEMS
We believe in using technology to address the absolute greatest challenges in the world. In the past year, we've already begun to experience extraordinary progress towards drastically improving the way global health is approached, and completely reworking the distribution of life-saving resources.
WHY ZENYSIS
Macro-level Impact
We build software for governments, building the tools decision makers use to affect 100+ million person populations. We work at the highest levels of government, redefining how aid gets delivered to millions of people suffering from preventable and curable illness.
Core Ownership
Still in the early stages, there’s tremendous room for growth, and opportunities to determine the direction of our product, operations and mission.
Immense Demand for your Work
In under a year, our product has been piloted in Ethiopia and South Africa and funded by major global health organizations such as the Gates Foundation and The Global Fund. We've also raised $2.8M from VCs including YC, Founders Fund, The Omidyar Network and 500 Startups.
