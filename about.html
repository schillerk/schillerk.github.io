THE WORLD IS CALLING
BUILT FOR THE DEVELOPING WORLD
Deploy Public Health Resources
Analysts and policy makers need tools to determine the proper allocation of the life-saving vaccines and resources. Through our platform, outbreaks can be tracked, controlled, and eliminated at the national level.
Target Preventable Disease
When Ethiopia was fighting a viral epidemic.  Our platform highlighted long-term hotspots and the most vulnerable segments of the population, allowing decision makers in the Ministry of Health to allocate millions of vaccines based on data.
Tailor Made
Zenysis is purpose-built for developing countries. We are not another contractor or prepackaged system. Our software is developed with direct input from our users on the ground and tested on their machines. For the first time, developing countries have software that is suited to their needs.
DESIGNED FOR IMPACT
Data Analytics at Scale
Our platform integrates over 300 million health records across over a dozen sources, each run by a separate entity, and built for a different purpose. For the first time in history, health officials in Ethiopia have the ability to understand problems across their entire country.
Seamless Data Integration
Once integrated, all data becomes accessible through a powerful data analytics interface, tailor-made for improving the decision making capabilities of health analysts. With improved models of interventions and the spread of disease, our clients will better understand relationships between investment and health outcomes.
Quantified and Tracked Efficacy
Subject to resource constraints, it's time for NGOs to get smarter about providing services to at-risk populations. By synthesizing data in a across fields such as HIV treatment, domestic violence, and education services, and pairing them with financials, we’re able to ensure that funding is allocated as efficiently as possible.
IMPLEMENTED ON THE GROUND
Users Guided by Deep Understanding
In addition to tools, we provide users with the training they need to make the most of our platform's capabilities. We're committed to creating a user base that is committed but independent, and empowered to make their own decisions.
Partner with Industry Leaders
We work with collaboration in mind, aiming to augment, rather than replace, the fantastic work already done in this field. We’re funded by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, work directly with the Ethiopian Ministry of Public Health, and work closely with organizations like The Global Fund, USAID and NACOSA.
Distribute the Future
Technology has done great things for developed countries, but there are 150 governments and 6 billion people whose quality of life can be vastly improved by the power of software. Zenysis brings startup technology, speed, and execution to serve governments and populations that are historically underserved.
